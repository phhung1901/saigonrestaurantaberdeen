About Us

A taste from the East never found in Aberdeen before.

Vietnamese cuisine has always had a healthy balance to its food. Prepared by using subtle seasonings and herbs, together with fresh vegetables, we endeavour to create a unique flavour that is lighter and more refreshing than either Thai or Chinese cooking.

Saigon have been preparing exquisite Vietnamese dishes for 15 years now.
